## What is DhivanSTech?

DhivanSTech (DST) is an electronics brand focused on two things: supplying quality maker components and test equipment, and documenting how to use them properly.

### What we sell

Everything in the DST catalogue falls into one of two categories:

- **DIY maker components** — breadboards, jumper wires, resistor kits, capacitor assortments, sensor modules, microcontroller boards, and other hands-on electronics parts
- **Test equipment** — multimeters, oscilloscopes, power supplies, and signal generators suited for bench work and field use

All products are sold via Amazon. We don't run our own store — Amazon handles fulfilment and returns. Our job is to select and list parts worth buying.

### What we document

The project blog covers real builds and experiments using the gear we sell. Posts are written in a direct engineering tone — what was built, why, what worked, and what didn't. No padding.

### Why DST?

Because most electronics listings on Amazon are low-information. It's hard to know what you're actually getting until it arrives. DST picks components with known specs, tests them where possible, and documents findings honestly.
