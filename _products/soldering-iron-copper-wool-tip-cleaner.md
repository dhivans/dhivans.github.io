This soldering iron copper wool tip cleaner is an essential tool for maintaining the efficiency of your soldering iron. This compact, handheld device features a metal handle with a pad of fine copper wool attached to the end. By gently rubbing the copper wool against the hot tip of your soldering iron, you can effectively remove excess solder, oxidation, and residue. This process helps to maintain the tip's shape and conductivity, ensuring efficient heat transfer and producing clean, reliable solder joints. With its simple design and ease of use, a soldering iron copper wool tip cleaner is an indispensable accessory for any soldering project, helping to extend the life and performance of your soldering iron.
